### BUG-05 – Pagination Freeze

**Severity:** High  

**Description:**  
Search results page becomes unresponsive when clicking on page 2.

**Steps to Reproduce:**  
1. Search for “Samsung”  
2. Scroll to pagination  
3. Click on page 2  

**Expected Result:**  
New page of results loads.

**Actual Result:**  
Page freezes and does not update.

**Evidence:**  
"Pagination freeze on Chrome – loader stuck"
