### BUG-03 – Broken Product Image

**Severity:** High  

**Description:**  
The main product image fails to load on the iPhone 15 product page.

**Steps to Reproduce:**  
1. Search for “iPhone 15”  
2. Click on the third product in the results  
3. Look at the main product image  

**Expected Result:**  
Product image loads correctly.

**Actual Result:**  
Product image does not load.

**Evidence:**  
"Image failed to load – broken file icon appears"
