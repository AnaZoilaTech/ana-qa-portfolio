### BUG-01 – Login Button Misaligned

**Severity:** Low  

**Description:**  
The login button is visually shifted to the left on the login page.

**Steps to Reproduce:**  
1. Open the MercadoLibre login page  
2. Observe the alignment of the login button  

**Expected Result:**  
The login button should be centered properly.

**Actual Result:**  
The login button appears shifted to the left.

**Evidence:**  
"Chrome latest version – button misaligned visually"
