### BUG-02 – Search Bar Placeholder Disappears Too Early

**Severity:** Medium  

**Description:**  
The placeholder text inside the search bar disappears immediately after clicking.

**Steps to Reproduce:**  
1. Open MercadoLibre homepage  
2. Click inside the search bar  
3. Observe the placeholder behavior  

**Expected Result:**  
Placeholder stays visible until the user starts typing.

**Actual Result:**  
Placeholder disappears before the user types.

**Evidence:**  
"Placeholder disappears on click – Chrome latest"
